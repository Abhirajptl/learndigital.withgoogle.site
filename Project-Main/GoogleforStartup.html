Google for startup Accelerator : Cloud

Google for Startups Accelerator offers three-month, equity-free accelerator programs for seed to Series A Indian technology startups. Accelerator: Cloud focuses on supporting growth-stage startups solving problems using advanced Cloud technology, such as AI/ML or data across key verticals. As part of this program, founders are paired with relevant Google Cloud and industry experts to solve their startup’s biggest challenges.
